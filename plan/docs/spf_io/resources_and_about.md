# spf.io Resources & About

This document provides background on the company, real-world impact through case studies, and a guide for new users.

## 1. About spf.io
Spf.io is an all-in-one AI platform for translation and accessibility, developed by **TheoTech LLC**. 

- **Mission**: To make every event, content, and conversation accessible and localized in real-time.
- **Vision**: Empowering organizations (churches, schools, corporations, and government) to reach global audiences without the high cost and complexity of traditional translation services.

---

## 2. Getting Started (Quick Start)
The onboarding process for new organizations typically follows these five steps:

1. **Request a Quote/Demo**: Engage with the spf.io team to define your needs and receive a custom pricing and account package.
2. **Choose Your Platform**: Decide where you want to deliver captions (Zoom, YouTube, In-Person, etc.).
3. **Configure Audio Capture**: Set up your audio source (Cloud Loopback or Local Virtual Audio Cable) so the AI engine can process the speech.
4. **Setup Translation Channels**: Assign your target languages and connect necessary API tokens (e.g., Zoom Caption token).
5. **Go Live**: Launch your session from the spf.io Operator Dashboard and share the viewing links with your audience.

## 3. Key Case Studies & Impact

- **Religious (FAC Radford)**: Successfully grew an inclusive multilingual ministry, helping non-English speakers feel a sense of belonging during worship.
- **Education (Google Digital Garage)**: Partnered to make online digital skills training accessible to a global audience via automated captions.
- **Corporate (Databricks DAIS 2024)**: Provided high-accuracy live captions for one of the world's largest data and AI conferences.
- **Community (Altoona Alliance Church)**: Built a welcoming environment for local international students and ESL learners through real-time translation.
