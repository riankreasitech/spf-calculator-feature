# spf.io Industry Solutions

Spf.io provides tailored accessibility and translation solutions for various sectors.

## 1. Religious / Church
Spf.io is widely used to create multilingual ministry environments.
- **Key Challenges**:
    - Making worship services accessible in dozens of languages simultaneously.
    - Translating sermon recordings for global reach.
    - Connecting diverse small groups (e.g., Bible studies) across language barriers.
- **Recommended Tools**: Live captions on big screens/mobile, sermon transcription, and "human-in-the-loop" editing for specialized religious terminology.
- **Notable Customers**: One Voice Fellowship, FAC Radford, Union Church.

## 2. Conferences & Summits
Scaling localization for international events cost-effectively.
- **Key Challenges**:
    - High cost of traditional human interpretation.
    - Maintaining accuracy for highly technical or niche terminology.
    - Engagement tools (Polls/Q&A) that work for all attendees.
- **Recommended Tools**: Real-time conference translation, multilingual Q&A, slide translation, and AI vocabulary fine-tuning.
- **Notable Customers**: CES 2025, Data + AI Summit (DAIS), Volvo, Panasonic.

## 3. Corporate Events
Ensuring internal and external communication is inclusive.
- **Key Challenges**:
    - Employee inclusion for global teams.
    - Accessibility compliance (ADA/International standards).
    - Managing hybrid meeting engagement.
- **Recommended Tools**: Virtual Interpreting (multi-booth setup), accessible slide translation, and a centralized accessibility management platform.
- **Notable Customers**: Accenture, Delta, Smartsheet.

## 4. Education
Supporting diverse classrooms and parent-teacher communication.
- **Key Challenges**:
    - Inclusion for ESL (English as a Second Language) students.
    - Communicating with parents who have limited English proficiency.
    - Creating interactive language learning materials from video content.
- **Recommended Tools**: Real-time lecture captions, media-to-lesson conversion tools, and parent-school meeting translation.

## 5. Government & Public Services
Equitable delivery of information to diverse communities.
- **Key Challenges**:
    - Reaching non-native speakers with critical public info.
    - Scalable accessibility for large public town halls.
- **Recommended Tools**: Scripted material automation (pre-loading speeches), live microphone-to-captioning, and mobile-based subtitle viewing.

## 6. Theater & Performing Arts
Revolutionizing live performance accessibility.
- **Key Challenges**:
    - Handling ad-libs or deviations from the script.
    - Content protection (preventing recording of captions).
    - Low-quality audio environments.
- **Recommended Tools**: **Script-to-Caption tool** (manual line release), "smart wrap" for readability, and instant switching between automated and script-based modes.
- **Notable Examples**: *Carla's Quince* (pioneering multilingual online play).
